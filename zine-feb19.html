February 2019

Various – Pop Makossa

2017 Analog Africa

Analog Africa smashing it as usual. Another brill compilation focusing on another hybridic African scene, this time Cameroonian dance music in the late 70s & early 80s. Everything on here is a banger. There’s all sorts of jams with acute guitars and cosmic synths shredding everywhere. The rhythms feel urgent and are tied to wickedly tight basslines. This compilation will make you move body parts. It collects a range of styles and tempos and still feels cohesive, much like recent favourites Space Echo & Digital Zandoli. Highlights include Nen Lambo, Africa and Sekele Movement. Bonus points for that lively cover.
